The Ackerman Model

Essential Concepts

  • Using "How" as "No"
  • Calibrated Questions
  • Nonround / Iregular numbers
  • Extreme anchors

How it works / Dialog Flow

reasons, reasons, reasons

  1. Set your target price (your goal buy/sell price)
  2. Set your first offer at 65% offset of your target price depending on if you are buying
    • If you are buying: offset
    • If you are selling: 200% - offset
  3. Calculate three price offset raises of decreasing increments (to 85%, 95%, and 100%).
  4. Use lots of empathy and different ways of saying "No" to get the other side to counter before you increase your offer.
  5. When calculating the final amount, use precise, nonround, numbers like, say, $37,893 rather than $38,000. It gives the number credibility and weight.
  6. On your final number, throw in a nonmonetary item (that they probably don't want) to show you're at your limit.
